About
Consortia is a creative office in New York City. We develop new frameworks for communication.

Our practice draws on research into emerging lifestyles and new ideas in art, architecture, technology, media, and digital culture.

We collaborate with a network of artists, designers, editors, and technologists to produce multidisciplinary strategies for brands and institutions.

Our projects combine physical activations and digital content to introduce new forms of knowledge and generate meaningful cultural impact.
